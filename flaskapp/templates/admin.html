Getting Started

Welcome to A Fresh Page! Getting started with our platform is easy. Follow these simple steps to navigate the website and make a meaningful contribution:

  1. Register an Account: If you haven't already, sign up for an account on our website. Registration is quick and free.
  2. Search for Foodbanks: Use our search feature to locate foodbanks near you. You can search by location or browse through the list of available options.
  3. Explore Foodbank Profiles: Click on the profiles of foodbanks to learn more about their mission, services, and the communities they serve.
  4. Select Your Preferred Foodbank: Once you've found a foodbank you'd like to support, select it as your preferred choice.
  5. Fill Out a Donation Request Form: Complete the donation request form provided on the foodbank's profile page. Specify the type and quantity of items you wish to donate.
  6. Submit Your Donation: After filling out the form, submit your donation request. The foodbank will review your request and provide further instructions for drop-off or collection.

Thank you for choosing A Fresh Page to make a difference in your community. Your contributions help us support those in need and create positive change.

Types of Donations Accepted

A Fresh Page LLC accepts a variety of donations to support foodbanks and those in need. Here are some of the items you can donate:

  • Non-perishable Food Items: Canned goods, rice, pasta, grains, and other shelf-stable foods.
  • Clothing: Gently used clothing for adults and children, including jackets, pants, shirts, and shoes.
  • Personal Hygiene Products: Feminine hygiene products like sanitary napkins, as well as toiletries such as soap, shampoo, and toothpaste.
  • Household Items: Basic household items such as blankets, towels, bedding, and kitchenware.
  • Financial Contributions: Monetary donations are also accepted to support the operational costs of foodbanks and their programs.

Your generosity helps ensure that individuals and families in your community have access to essential items and support during times of need.
